Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

20Focus ("we", "our", or "the app") is a timer app that helps you follow the 20-20-20 rule for eye health. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All app data is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

Specifically:

  • No account creation or login required
  • No names, email addresses, or contact information collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No location data
  • No cookies or device identifiers

Locally Stored Data

The app stores the following data on your device only:

  • Your timer settings (work interval, break duration, preferences)
  • Break statistics (number of breaks taken today and all time)
  • Notification schedule preferences

This data is stored using Apple's on-device storage and is never sent to any server. You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app.

Subscriptions

20Focus offers subscription-based access through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details. For information about how Apple handles your purchase data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit for subscription management. Apple processes transactions according to their own privacy policy.
  • RevenueCat for subscription analytics and management. RevenueCat may receive anonymized transaction data from Apple. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

We do not use any advertising SDKs, analytics frameworks, or social media trackers.

Notifications

The app may send local notifications to remind you to take eye breaks. These notifications are generated entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any server. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

20Focus does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal data from any user, no special provisions are necessary.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
